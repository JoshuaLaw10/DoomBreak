🧠 Privacy Policy

Doomscroll Break  ·  Last updated: 2026-05-24

Doomscroll Break stores a small amount of data locally on your device only. No data is ever transmitted to any server. No analytics. No tracking. No third-party services.

What data is stored

All data is stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local — a sandboxed, device-local key-value store provided by Chrome. It is never synced, shared, or transmitted.

Storage keyWhat it holdsWhy
enabledBoolean — extension on/offPersists your preference across sessions
soundOnBoolean — video sound on/offPersists your mute preference
promptModeBoolean — prompt-aware modePersists your content preference
sloganIndexInteger — slogan rotation positionKeeps slogans from repeating on restart
autoCloseStreakObject — auto-close count per day (7-day window)Powers the daily streak counter in the overlay
selectorTelemetryObject — per-selector match timestamps & countsPowers the popup health check. Never leaves your device.

What data is NOT collected

  • No personal information
  • No browsing history
  • No prompt or conversation content
  • No usage analytics
  • No crash reports
  • No identifiers of any kind

Network access

Doomscroll Break makes zero network requests. All video clips are bundled with the extension as local MP4 files. The content script observes the ChatGPT DOM to detect generation state — it does not send any data to or from those pages.

Permissions

PermissionWhy it's needed
storageTo read and write the preference and telemetry keys listed above
content script on chatgpt.com / gemini.google.comThe content script must run on chatgpt.com to observe the DOM and detect generation state

Data retention

autoCloseStreak entries older than 7 days are automatically pruned. selectorTelemetry is capped at 32 entries. All data can be cleared at any time via Chrome → Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Site data, or by uninstalling the extension.
